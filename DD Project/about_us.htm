About Us


Dee Dee's is the leading hair & beauty salon and academy in Silchar having two outlets: one at Premtala Point and the newly one at Goldighi Municipality Mall. It is owned by Mr. Debashis Das and Mrs. Papiya Das, and is registered under Director of Industries, District Industries Centre, Government of Assam, bearing the registration no. 020105509 dated 17-11-95.

It has been 20 years since we are satisfying our customers. We have adopted the science of understanding and styling hair and not merely cutting or using styling products. All our employees are well trained to benefit our customers. Our state-of-art infrastructure makes styling more comfortable and brings smiles to our customers. We leave no stones unturned to delight our customers and not only this, we also take feed back from them as an opportunity to improve our skills.

We love and respect our colleagues, our well-wishers, our dealers and all those who are attached with us in our success over the years. We make our work place a happy work place. We take ownership of everything around us and constantly strive to improve the standards of service and quality of deliverance.
